About us.

SRMKZILLA is a community of young tech enthusiasts who eat, sleep and breath technology. We organize everything from technical workshops to gaming events, you name it & we do it. We are firm believers of growing and learning together because together, we achieve more.

SRMKZILLA regurlarly organises workshops,technical talks and discussions,meetups to spread the message of the power of open sourcet to fellow students. We also organize Mozofest - a free for all access fest where we call in speakers and experts from the community to teach about the latest developments in open source and we also organise games and coding events where people can test their skill.

Values

As the saying goes, “There’s no I in a team.” We understand the importance of giving and contributing to the community over our personal time.

We believe in thinking big, but taking small steps. We try new things, but without losing vision of what really matters.

We are committed to our work and will keep on going despite hardships, inconveniences or interruptions.

We grow as we learn. We acquire ideas, experiences and the perspective from all to get the best out of us.

Mission

As a part of Mozilla's open source community, our mission is to create a platform where individuals can shape their own experience and are empowered, safe and independent.

Mentor aspirants to unleash their potential, towards nation building.

To exhibit professional ethics, commitment and leadership qualities

To empower the students with the required skills to solve the complex technological problems of modern society.

Vision

A community that innovates, investigates and discovers the true potential of aspirants.

To inculcate the necessary values to sustain in constantly changing technical environment.

To effectuate supremacy in technical education through workshops, projects and training.
